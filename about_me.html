About Me

I am a computer engineer graduate from Syracuse University (Class of 2020). I chose to pursue Computer Engineering Masters program after finishing my Bachelor's in Electronics Engineering as in my senior year I had the opportunity to work with drones; a perfect combination of software and hardware for me.
Being from an electronic discipline and working on an autonomous drone for wildfire detection, I realized how much I lack in programming knowledge. It was then I decided to put extra hours outside my academics to learn programming. I drove a 3 man team in giving shape to the project from conception to the final product.
With all the excitement to learn and explore software discipline, I studied advanced computer architecture, object-oriented design, advanced algorithms, and data structures with database management and internet programming during my master's program.
I was hired at Syracuse Center of Excellence and was assigned to work on an IoT project. I had to integrate multiple sensors and develop an application to control the device with the ability of data acquisition. During the summer, I not only learned new tech stack I had to work on, but delivered an efficient and robust functioning prototype.
Outside all technical endeavors, I honed my communication and public speaking skills while hosting multiple tech-talks and events in my undergraduate college as head of the entrepreneurship cell. Additionally, I maintained my mother's business website and youtube channel to help her brand, Saksham, grow online. Today she is India's top digital leader in her company.
